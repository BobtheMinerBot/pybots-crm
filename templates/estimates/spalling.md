## Pricing Calculator

**Basic Formula:**
- Small job (<50 sq ft): $75-100/sq ft all-in
- Medium job (50-150 sq ft): $55-75/sq ft all-in
- Large job (>150 sq ft): $45-60/sq ft all-in

**Add for complexity:**
- Elevated work (>10 ft): +20%
- Structural concerns: +15-25%
- Difficult access: +10-15%
- Decorative finish matching: +15%

## Scope Description Template

```
SCOPE OF WORK: Concrete Spalling Repair

ISLA Builders will perform the following repairs at [ADDRESS]:

1. PREPARATION
   - Establish work area protection with plastic sheeting
   - Set up required scaffolding/ladders for access
   - Mark all areas requiring repair

2. CONCRETE REMOVAL
   - Remove all loose, damaged, and deteriorated concrete
   - Sound concrete removal to [X]" beyond visible damage
   - Expose and clean all corroded reinforcement

3. REBAR TREATMENT
   - Wire brush all exposed reinforcement to bright metal
   - Apply rust-inhibiting primer to all exposed steel
   - Install supplemental reinforcement as needed

4. CONCRETE REPAIR
   - Apply bonding agent to prepared surfaces
   - Install polymer-modified repair mortar in layers as needed
   - Form and pour sections requiring >2" depth

5. FINISHING
   - Match existing concrete texture/finish
   - Apply penetrating waterproof sealer
   - [Optional: Apply elastomeric coating]

6. CLEANUP
   - Remove all debris and materials
   - Final inspection with owner

AREAS OF REPAIR:
[List specific columns/beams/areas with measurements]

TIMELINE: [X] working days from start
WARRANTY: [X] year workmanship warranty
```
